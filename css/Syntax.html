HASSAAN ALI

11-year-old boy is giving lessons to BTech and MTech students in Hyderabad,
Andhra Pradesh. Schoolkid Mohammed Hassan Ali is a wonder boy who teaches students much older to him
who are pursuing engineering at bachelor's and master's level.
Ali is a Class 8 student. He does not charge any fees for the lessons he gives to college students.
Ali wants to teach a thousand engineers by the end of 2020.
